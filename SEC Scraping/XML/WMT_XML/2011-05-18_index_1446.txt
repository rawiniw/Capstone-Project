<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Dear Wal-Mart Stores Shareowner:</font></div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">On behalf of the New York City Pension Funds (NYC Funds), <font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">I urge you to vote FOR Proposal No. 8 &#8211; Require Supplier(s) to Publish an Annual Sustainability Report&#8212;at Wal-Mart Stores&#8217; 2011 Annual Meeting on Friday, June 3, 2011.</font></font></div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">The proposal calls on Wal-Mart&#8217;s Board of Directors to require the Company&#8217;s suppliers to publish annually an independently verifiable sustainability report. Among other important disclosures, a report would include a supplier&#8217;s objective assessment and measurement of performance on workplace safety and human and worker rights, using internationally recognized standards, indicators and measurement protocols. In addition, a report would include incidents of non-compliance, actions taken to remedy those incidents, and measures taken to contribute to long-term prevention and mitigation.</font></div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">The Securities and Exchange Commission rejected Wal-Mart&#8217;s arguments that it could exclude the proposal from its Proxy Statement on the grounds that it relates to the Company&#8217;s ordinary business operations; or that the Company had already substantially implemented the proposal.</font></div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">The NYC Funds collectively hold 5.7 million shares of Wal-Mart common stock, valued at approximately $316 million, as of May 11, 2011.&#160;&#160;As New York City Comptroller, I serve as investment advisor, custodian and a trustee for the NYC Funds.</font></div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">As long-term investors, the NYC Funds affirm that the global integration of sustainability into business strategies and cultures is critically needed for the protection and enhancement of long-term investments and sustainable economic prosperity. However, the failure of suppliers to integrate sustainability into their strategic plans and culture poses a major impediment to the achievement of this important goal. Consider the following:</font></div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Given Wal-Mart&#8217;s apparent inadequate monitoring of its suppliers&#8217; compliance with its &#8220;Standards for Suppliers,&#8221; the long-term interests of Wal-Mart, its shareholders and suppliers would be better served if Wal-Mart requires its suppliers to publish annual, independently verifiable, sustainability reports. This approach derives from the old adage, &#8220;What Gets Measured Gets Done.&#8221; The independently verifiable reporting requirement would drive suppliers to establish performance goals and benchmarks that would help to integrate sustainability, including workplace safety, and human and worker rights, into their business strategies and cultures.</font></div>
