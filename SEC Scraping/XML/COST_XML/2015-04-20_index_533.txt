<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>COSTCO WHOLESALE CORPORATION ANNOUNCES REAUTHORIZATION OF ITS STOCK REPURCHASE PROGRAM AND AN INCREASE IN ITS QUARTERLY CASH DIVIDEND
</B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">ISSAQUAH, Wash., April&nbsp;17, 2015 &#150; Costco Wholesale Corporation (&#147;Costco&#148; or the &#147;Company&#148;) (Nasdaq: COST) today
announced that its Board of Directors has reauthorized a common stock repurchase program of up to $4 billion. This program will expire in April 2019 and replaces the current $4 billion program (expiring this month), which has unused authorization of
approximately $2.5 billion. The Board of Directors also declared a quarterly cash dividend on Costco common stock and approved a quarterly increase from $.355 to $.40 per share, or $1.60 per share on an annualized basis. The dividend is payable
May&nbsp;15, 2015, to shareholders of record at the close of business on May&nbsp;1, 2015. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Costco currently operates 672 warehouses, including 474 in the
United States and Puerto Rico, 89 in Canada, 34 in Mexico, 26 in the United Kingdom, 20 in Japan, 11 in Korea, 10 in Taiwan, seven in Australia and one in Spain. The Company plans to open up to an additional 16 new warehouses (including one
relocation to a larger and better-located facility) prior to the end of its fiscal year on August&nbsp;30, 2015. Costco also operates electronic commerce web sites in the U.S., Canada, the United Kingdom and Mexico. </P>
