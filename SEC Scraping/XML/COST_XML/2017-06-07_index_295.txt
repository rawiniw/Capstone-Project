 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>Explanatory Note </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">This Current Report on Form 8-K/A (the &#147;Amendment&#148;) is being filed as an amendment to the Current Report on Form 8-K filed by Costco
Wholesale Corporation (the &#147;Company&#148;) on February&nbsp;1, 2017 (the &#147;Original 8-K&#148;). The Original 8-K was filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission to report the results of the matters submitted to a vote of the
shareholders at the at the Company&#146;s 2017 Annual Meeting of Shareholders, held on January&nbsp;26, 2017. The sole purpose of this Amendment is to disclose, in accordance with Item&nbsp;5.07(d) of Form 8-K, the Company&#146;s decision as to how
frequently the Company will conduct future shareholder advisory votes regarding named executive compensation. Except as set forth herein, no other modifications have been made to the Original 8-K. </P>
<P STYLE="font-size:18pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>

<TD WIDTH="9%" VALIGN="top" ALIGN="left"><B>Item&nbsp;5.07</B></TD>
<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"><B>Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders </B></TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">(d) At the Annual Meeting, the
Company&#146;s shareholders voted on, among other matters, an advisory proposal concerning the frequency of future advisory votes on executive compensation. As reported in the Original 8-K, the advisory vote was in favor of annual frequency. In
light of the Company&#146;s prior practice of annual advisory votes on executive compensation and the vote of the shareholders, the Company intends to continue the practice of holding the votes annually. </P>
