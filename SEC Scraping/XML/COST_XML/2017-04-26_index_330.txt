<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>QUARTERLY CASH DIVIDEND </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">ISSAQUAH, Wash.,
April&nbsp;25, 2017 - Costco Wholesale Corporation (&#147;Costco&#148; or the &#147;Company&#148;) (Nasdaq: COST) announced today that its Board of Directors has declared a special cash dividend on Costco common stock of $7.00 per share, payable
May&nbsp;26, 2017, to shareholders of record at the close of business on May&nbsp;10, 2017. The aggregate payment will be approximately $3.1&nbsp;billion. The special dividend will be funded primarily through additional borrowings. The special
dividend will be in addition to the Company&#146;s regular quarterly cash dividend of 50 cents per share that was also declared today, which will also be payable on May&nbsp;26, 2017, to shareholders of record at the close of business on
May&nbsp;12, 2017. That amount represents a quarterly increase of 5 cents per share. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Richard Galanti, Executive Vice President and Chief Financial
Officer, commented: &#147;Today&#146;s announcement of a $7.00 special dividend is our latest step in returning capital to our shareholders. Our strong balance sheet and favorable access to the credit markets allow us to provide shareholders with
this dividend, while preserving financial and operational flexibility to continue to grow our business globally. Costco will continue to be in a financial position allowing us to take care of our employees, enhance the value of the Costco
membership, and create shareholder value over the long term.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Costco currently operates 729 warehouses, including 508 in the United States and
Puerto Rico, 95 in Canada, 37 in Mexico, 28 in the United Kingdom, 25 in Japan, 13 in Korea, 13 in Taiwan, eight in Australia and two in Spain. Costco also operates electronic commerce web sites in the U.S., Canada, the United Kingdom, Mexico, Korea
