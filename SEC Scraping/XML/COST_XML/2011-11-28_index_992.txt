 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Item&nbsp;5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain
Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:8%"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">(e) As previously disclosed, effective January&nbsp;1, 2012, Craig
Jelinek will become President and Chief Executive Officer of the Company. On November&nbsp;23, 2011, the Compensation Committee of the Company&#146;s Board of Directors determined the compensation arrangements for Mr.&nbsp;Jelinek in his new
position. He will receive an annual base salary of $650,000 prorated from January&nbsp;1, 2012 through September&nbsp;2, 2012, and he will be eligible for a bonus of up to $200,000. He will continue to receive his current employee benefits.
</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Mr.&nbsp;Jelinek previously received an award of 50,000 RSUs for fiscal 2012, which is intended to qualify as
&#147;performance-based&#148; compensation under applicable tax law and is subject to specified performance criteria for fiscal 2012. The performance targets may be satisfied by the Company&#146;s achieving specified percentage increases, relative
to fiscal 2011, in either total sales or pre-tax income for fiscal 2012. If the Committee determines that either or both of these targets have been met, then the RSUs will be granted following the end of the Company&#146;s fiscal year and will be
subject to further vesting thereafter based on the executive&#146;s continued employment with the Company through October 2015, with 20% of the shares vesting in October of each year beginning in 2012. </FONT></P>
