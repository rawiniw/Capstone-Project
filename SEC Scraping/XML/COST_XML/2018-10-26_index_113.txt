 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="right">Exhibit 10.1 </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center">Costco Wholesale Corporation </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center">Executive Bonus Plan </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">For fiscal year 2019,
executive officers are, depending upon the individual, eligible for a bonus award of up to approximately $200,000. The final bonus amounts are subject to the approval of the Compensation Committee. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Eligibility for up to 50% of the bonus amount is determined by whether the Company has met its <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">pre-tax</FONT> income goal for
the year, as indicated in the Company&#146;s internal budget. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The remaining 50% is determined by goals relevant to the executive officer&#146;s area of
responsibility and a discretionary component determined by the individual&#146;s manager: </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; margin-left:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">For those whose responsibilities are
operational, the goals relate to sales, controllable expense, shrink, and <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">pre-tax</FONT> profit in their areas of responsibility. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; margin-left:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">For those whose responsibilities are primarily buying, the goals relate to sales and <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">pre-shrink</FONT> gross
margin in their areas of responsibility. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; margin-left:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">For those whose responsibilities are staff functions, the goals relate to a combination of
Company-wide operational and buying goals, in addition to qualitative factors relevant to their areas of responsibility. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">To be eligible for the annual
bonus, an individual must still be employed by the Company at the time bonus checks are issued (November). </P>
