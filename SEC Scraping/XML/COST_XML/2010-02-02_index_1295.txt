 <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>COSTCO WHOLESALE CORPORATION ANNOUNCES EXECUTIVE MANAGEMENT CHANGES </B></FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">ISSAQUAH, Wash., February&nbsp;1, 2010 &#150; Costco Wholesale Corporation (Nasdaq: COST) announced today the appointment by the Board of Directors of Craig
Jelinek as President and Chief Operating Officer, effective immediately. Mr.&nbsp;Jelinek previously was Executive Vice President in charge of merchandising, a position he has held since 2004. He spent the previous twenty years in various management
positions in warehouse operations. Mr.&nbsp;Jelinek will join the Board of Directors. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Jim Sinegal will continue as Chief Executive Officer of
the Company. Mr.&nbsp;Sinegal, together with Chairman of the Board Jeff Brotman, Senior Executive Vice President Dick DiCerchio and Mr.&nbsp;Jelinek, will form an Office of the President, through which the four will coordinate on major Company
matters. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Mr.&nbsp;Sinegal stated: &#147;We are excited about Craig&#146;s elevation to his new role. He is a highly seasoned retail
executive, with over twenty-five years&#146; experience at our Company and over thirty-five years&#146; experience in the industry. We look forward to his participation in a broader range of decisions as we move the Company forward.&#148;
</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Costco currently operates 566 warehouses, including 413 in the United States and Puerto Rico, 77 in Canada, 21 in the United Kingdom, seven
in Korea, six in Taiwan, nine in Japan, 32 in Mexico and one in Australia. The Company also operates Costco Online, an electronic commerce web site, at www.costco.com and at www.costco.ca in Canada. </FONT></P> <P
