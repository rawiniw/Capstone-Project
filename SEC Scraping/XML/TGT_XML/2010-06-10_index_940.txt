the votes cast with respect to the director, provided that directors shall be

election of directors at any such meeting for which the number of nominees

corporation first mails its notice for such meeting to the shareholders)

For purposes of this Article&nbsp;VI, action at a meeting shall mean
action at a meeting which satisfies the notice and quorum requirements imposed

majority of the votes cast means that the votes entitled to be cast by the
holders of all then outstanding shares of Voting Stock (as defined in Article&nbsp;V)
