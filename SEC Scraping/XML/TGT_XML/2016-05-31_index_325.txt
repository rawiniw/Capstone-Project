<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" color="#cc0000" face="Arial" style="color:#CC0000;font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Introduction</font></b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">This Conflict Minerals Report is being filed by Target Corporation (&#147;Target&#148;) pursuant to Rule&nbsp;13p-1 under the Securities Exchange Act and Form&nbsp;SD (collectively, the &#147;Conflict Minerals Rule&#148;). As used in this report, consistent with the Conflict Minerals Rule, &#147;3TG&#148; means columbite-tantalite (coltan), cassiterite, gold, wolframite and the derivatives tantalum, tin, and tungsten.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" color="#c00000" face="Arial" style="color:#C00000;font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">A.&#160; Applicability of the Conflict Minerals Rule&nbsp;to Target</font></b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">Target is a retailer selling both everyday essentials and fashionable, differentiated merchandise at discounted prices. Target does not manufacture any of the products it sells. A significant portion of Target&#146;s sales is from national brand merchandise. Approximately one-third of Target&#146;s fiscal 2015 sales related to owned and exclusive brand products. Target may be subject to the requirements of the Conflict Minerals Rule&nbsp;because of the degree of influence that it may exercise over the materials, parts, ingredients, or components of some of its owned and exclusive brand products that contain necessary 3TG.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">For 2015, for purposes of its compliance with the Conflict Minerals Rule, Target had potentially in-scope products in the owned and exclusive brand merchandise categories in the table below (the &#147;Surveyed Product Categories&#148;). As discussed herein, for 2015, Target&#146;s outreach included all of the vendors </font><font face="Arial">of products that were identified as possibly containing 3TG<font color="black" style="color:black;"> within the Surveyed Product Categories (the &#147;Surveyed Vendors&#148;), regardless of whether the products of a particular vendor were believed to contain necessary 3TG and regardless of whether Target may have contracted to manufacture those products.</font></font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">For 2015, Target was unable to determine the origin of at least a portion of the necessary 3TG contained in the products of Surveyed Vendors. In some instances, Target was able to determine that some Surveyed Vendors for 2015 did not source from the Democratic Republic of the Congo (the &#147;DRC&#148;) or an adjoining country. Target&#146;s findings about the source of necessary 3TG in products from Surveyed Vendors for 2015 were limited by the fact that some vendors did not respond to Target&#146;s survey and that most vendors did not provide information about the source of necessary 3TG on an individual product basis.</font></p>

<br clear="all" style="page-break-before:always;">
<div>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">Target does not do any direct business with any smelters or refiners of 3TG. As a &#147;downstream&#148; company (which means a company between the smelter or refiner and the consumer), it must rely on its vendors to provide accurate, reliable information about 3TG in its supply chain. Target also believes that, in most cases, it is several or more vendor tiers removed from the smelters and refiners of 3TG used in its products. However, through its leadership in the retail industry, and through the efforts described in this Conflict Minerals Report, including its support of multi-stakeholder initiatives, Target is helping to address some of those difficulties presented to downstream companies and to encourage responsible sourcing in its supply chain.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" color="#c00000" face="Arial" style="color:#C00000;font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">B.&#160; Target&#146;s Conflict Minerals Policy; Participation in Industry Initiatives</font></b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">Target supports the humanitarian goals of the Conflict Minerals Rule&nbsp;and recognizes the adverse impact from mining and trade of 3TG by armed groups in the DRC and its adjoining countries. Target seeks neither, directly nor indirectly, to finance or benefit those armed groups.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">Target also recognizes that there are many smelters and refiners that source from the DRC and its adjoining countries whose activities do not finance or benefit armed groups and who are certified as &#147;compliant&#148; or the equivalent by a recognized independent certification organization. In order to avoid the undue harm to local populations that a generalized embargo upon this region could perform, Target affirmatively encourages its suppliers to identify compliant smelters and refiners within the DRC and its adjoining countries and to source from them when commercially practicable.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">Target&#146;s conflict minerals policy (the &#147;Conflict Minerals Policy&#148;) seeks to balance Target&#146;s objectives of ensuring that its business activities do not finance or benefit armed groups and providing support to compliant smelters and refiners in the DRC and its adjoining countries. The Conflict Minerals Policy states that Target will not knowingly purchase or sell any owned or exclusive brand product that contains 3TG that finances armed conflict in the DRC or an adjoining country. The Conflict Minerals Policy also affirmatively encourages Target&#146;s suppliers to source from smelters and refiners within the DRC and its adjoining countries that are certified as compliant by an independent certification organization.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">The Conflict Minerals Policy also includes, but is not limited to, Target&#146;s expectation that each of the vendors with which it contracts to manufacture will:</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:10.0pt;">1.</font><font size="1" face="Arial" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;">Adopt a policy relating to 3TG sourcing that is consistent with Target&#146;s Conflict Minerals Policy and the<i> OECD Due Diligence Guidance for Responsible Supply Chains of Minerals from Conflict-Affected and High-Risk Areas: Third Edition</i> (the &#147;OECD Due Diligence Guidance&#148;);</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Target&#146;s policy requires the maintenance of its 3TG-related records for a minimum of six years. Target has requested that the Service Provider store records in its possession on Target&#146;s behalf in accordance with Target&#146;s internal document retention policy.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">Target communicates its sourcing expectations relating to 3TG to vendors through direct communications, information posted on Target&#146;s vendor website, and online training. Target&#146;s item set-up system requests vendors to specify whether a vendor&#146;s items contain 3TG. Target&#146;s standard contract requires vendors to comply with the objectives of Target&#146;s Conflict Minerals Policy. 3TG training </font><font face="Arial">is made available to new vendors <font color="black" style="color:black;">as part of Target&#146;s onboarding process</font> and remains available to all vendors after onboarding. <font color="black" style="color:black;">Additionally, vendors are provided with an email address at Target where they can direct any questions about Target&#146;s 3TG compliance program or Conflict Minerals Policy.</font></font></p>

The program requires those vendors to register all applicable facilities with Target, indicating the locations of facilities. Additionally, every facility and vendor where owned and exclusive brand production takes place must authorize unannounced compliance audits. Through Target&#146;s broader social compliance program, Target has the right to conduct unannounced spot-checks of vendors who may have produced products that contain 3TG and have access to their documentation.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Step 2.&#160; Identify and Assess Risks in the Supply Chain</font></b></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">Through the Service Provider, Target requests that Surveyed Vendors</font><font face="Arial"> complete a C<font color="black" style="color:black;">onflict Minerals Reporting Template. The Conflict Minerals Reporting Template allows Surveyed Vendors to provide product-specific information.</font></font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">The Service Provider sends reminders to each non-responsive Surveyed Vendor requesting registration with the Service Provider&#146;s system and completion of a Conflict Minerals Reporting Template. If a Surveyed Vendor continues to be non-responsive, the Service Provider is required to notify Target. TSSQC team members then follow up with each non-responsive Surveyed Vendor and request the Surveyed Vendor&#146;s participation.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">The Service Provider reviews the responses received from the Surveyed Vendors against specific quality control flags that trigger follow-up or escalation of the response. If a Surveyed Vendor with products that contain 3TG does not identify the smelters or refiners of 3TG in its Conflict Minerals Reporting Template, the Service Provider attempts to obtain information about the suppliers of 3TG for the Surveyed Vendor&#146;s products by making inquiries of the Surveyed Vendor&#146;s identified suppliers. Follow-up inquiries are made by the Service Provider to Surveyed Vendors to address other incomplete responses or where Surveyed Vendors indicate sources of their 3TG that are unknown to the Service Provider.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">If an entity identified as a smelter or refiner by a Surveyed Vendor is confirmed by the Service Provider to be a smelter or refiner, the Service Provider examines whether the smelter or refiner was certified as &#147;compliant&#148; or the equivalent by the CFSI, the London Bullion Market Association (the &#147;LBMA&#148;) or the Responsible Jewelry Council (the &#147;RJC&#148;). If the identified smelter or refiner is not certified by one of these independent third parties, the Service Provider attempts to contact the smelter or refiner and/or consult publicly available information to attempt to determine the mine or location of origin of the necessary 3TG processed by the smelter or refiner.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Step 3.&#160; Design and Implement a Strategy to Respond to Identified Risks</font></b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">Summaries of Surveyed Vendor responses are provided to the leadership of TSSQC. If a Surveyed Vendor response indicates that a product Target contracted to manufacture contained 3TG from the DRC or an adjoining country that was not verified as coming from a compliant smelter or refiner, Target&#146;s 3TG compliance procedures specify that leadership of TSSQC will be notified and the Surveyed Vendor contacted. Purchases of the identified product will be suspended until the Surveyed Vendor can locate a source of 3TG that can either be verified as coming from outside the DRC or an adjoining country or from a compliant smelter or refiner in the DRC or an adjoining country, subject to a reasonable grace period to allow the existing smelter or refiner to become compliant. TSSQC will track any future violations of Target&#146;s policy by Surveyed Vendors and stop doing business with any Surveyed Vendor that is unwilling or unable to comply with Target&#146;s policy.</font></p>
