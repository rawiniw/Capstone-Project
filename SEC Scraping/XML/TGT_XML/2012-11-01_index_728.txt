On this same basis, October&nbsp;comparable-store sales increased 2.4 percent.</p>

With programs like 5% REDcard Rewards and Holiday Price Match our guests can shop at Target with confidence &#150; knowing they&#146;re getting the best prices on a unique assortment of holiday wants and needs.&#148;</font></p>

The 14 cent difference between these ranges reflected the expected EPS impact of expenses related to the company&#146;s Canadian market launch. Beyond the impact of its Canadian market launch, Target now expects two factors to benefit its third quarter 2012 GAAP EPS (both will be excluded from Target&#146;s adjusted EPS calculation):</font></p>
