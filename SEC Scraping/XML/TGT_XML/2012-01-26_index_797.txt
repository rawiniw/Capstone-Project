<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.0in;text-indent:-1.0in;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Item 1.02.</font></b><b><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;font-weight:bold;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font></b><b><u><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Termination of a Material Definitive Agreement</font></u></b><b><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">.</font></b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.0in;text-indent:-1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On January&nbsp;26, 2012, TCC Corporation S.&#224; r.l. (&#147;TCC&#148;), a </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">wholly owned subsidiary of Target Corporation (&#147;Target&#148;), purchased the Floating Rate Asset-Backed Note (the &#147;Note&#148;), issued by the Target Credit Card Owner Trust 2008-1 (&#147;Trust&#148;), from JPMN II Inc. f/k/a BOTAC,&nbsp;Inc. (&#147;JPMN&#148;), an affiliate of Chase Bank USA, National Association (&#147;Chase&#148;).&#160; The purchase price for the Note was approximately $2.77 billion, along with a make-whole premium of approximately $85 million.&#160; The Note was cancelled after TCC&#146;s purchase of the Note.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">In connection with the purchase and cancellation of the Note, the following agreements and the Trust itself have been terminated:</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Note Purchase Agreement (the &#147;Note Purchase Agreement&#148;) dated May&nbsp;5, 2008, as amended, among JPMN, Chase, Target and Target Receivables LLC f/k/a Target Receivables Corporation (&#147;TRLLC&#148;), a wholly owned subsidiary of Target;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Indenture (the &#147;Indenture&#148;) dated May&nbsp;19, 2008, as amended, between the Trust, as issuer, and Wells Fargo Bank, National Association, as indenture trustee (in such capacity, the &#147;Indenture Trustee&#148;); and</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Series&nbsp;2008-1 Supplement (the &#147;Series&nbsp;2008-1 Supplement&#148;) dated May&nbsp;19, 2008, as amended, to the Amended and Restated Pooling and Servicing Agreement dated April&nbsp;28, 2000, as amended, among TRLLC, Target National Bank and Wells Fargo Bank, National Association, as trustee.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The Note was issued by the Trust pursuant to the Note Purchase Agreement in a transaction to sell an undivided interest in Target&#146;s credit card receivables to JPMN.&#160; The Note was backed by credit card receivables held in the Target Credit Card Master Trust through an investor certificate issued by TRLLC pursuant to the Series&nbsp;2008-1 Supplement and deposited by TRLLC into the Trust.&#160; The investor certificate was in turn pledged by the Trust to the Indenture Trustee to secure the Note.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A. (&#147;JPMorgan&#148;), an affiliate of Chase and JPMN, and certain of JPMorgan&#146;s affiliates have performed and may in the future perform various commercial banking and investment banking services for Target.</font></p>
