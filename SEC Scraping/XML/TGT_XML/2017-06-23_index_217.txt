<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:24.5pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Target Corporation (the &#147;Company&#148;) is filing this Post-Effective Amendment No.&nbsp;1 to Form&nbsp;S-8 Registration Statement to deregister certain securities originally registered by the Company pursuant to its Registration Statements on Form&nbsp;S-8 filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on August&nbsp;29, 2008 (Registration Statement No.&nbsp;333-153250), January&nbsp;18, 2006 (Registration Statement No.&nbsp;333-131083), March&nbsp;19, 2003 (Registration Statement No.&nbsp;333-103920), May&nbsp;19, 1997 (Registration Statement No.&nbsp;333-27435) and July&nbsp;15, 1993 (Registration Statement No.&nbsp;33-66050, collectively referred to herein as the &#147;Prior Registration Statements&#148;) with respect to shares of the Company&#146;s common stock, par value $.0833 per share (the &#147;Common Stock&#148;), thereby registered for offer or sale pursuant to the Target Corporation 401(k)&nbsp;Plan (formerly known as the Dayton Hudson Supplemental Retirement, Savings and Employee Stock Ownership Plan), as amended and restated (the &#147;Plan&#148;). The Prior Registration Statements registered a total of 339,200,000 shares of Common Stock, as adjusted for stock splits. The Prior Registration Statements also registered an indeterminate amount of interests to be offered or sold pursuant to the Plan.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:24.5pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Pursuant to the undertakings contained in the Prior Registration Statements to remove from registration, by means of a post-effective amendment, any of the securities registered that remain unissued at the termination of the offering, the Company is filing this post-effective amendment to the Prior Registration Statements to deregister, and does hereby remove from registration, 2,661,427 shares of Common Stock and all Plan participation interests that had been registered under the Prior Registration Statements that remain unissued as of the date hereof.</font></p>
