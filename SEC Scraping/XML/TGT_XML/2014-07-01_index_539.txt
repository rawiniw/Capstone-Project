<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;punctuation-wrap:simple;text-indent:.5in;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">MINNEAPOLIS (July&nbsp;1, 2014) </font></b><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#151; Target Corporation (&#147;<u>Target</u>&#148;) (NYSE: TGT) today announced the consideration payable in connection with its previously announced tender offers (the &#147;<u>Tender Offers</u>&#148;), which commenced on June&nbsp;17, 2014 to purchase up to $1,000,000,000 in aggregate purchase price (the &#147;<u>Maximum Payment Amount</u>&#148;) of its notes or debentures listed in the table below (collectively, the &#147;<u>Notes</u>&#148; and each a &#147;<u>series</u>&#148; of Notes).&#160; The Tender Offers will expire at 11:59&nbsp;p.m., New York City time, on July&nbsp;15, 2014, unless extended (such date and time, as the same may be extended, the &#147;<u>Expiration Date</u>&#148;).&#160; The Tender Offers are being made solely pursuant to the offer to purchase and related letter of transmittal, each dated June&nbsp;17, 2014 (as they may be amended or supplemented, the &#147;<u>Tender Offer Documents</u>&#148;).&#160; Target announced the initial results of the Tender Offers on June&nbsp;30, 2014.</font></p>
