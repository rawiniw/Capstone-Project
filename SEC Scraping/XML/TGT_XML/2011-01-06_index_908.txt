Our 5% REDcard Rewards program is delivering the results we expected, and we&#146;re confident that we will continue to generate profitable growth, even while consumer buying patterns exhibit volatility across categories and over time.&#148;</font></p>

The company also believes that the current median First Call estimate of $1.40 for Target&#146;s fourth quarter earnings per share is a reasonable estimate within a range of possible outcomes, as favorability in the corporation&#146;s credit card segment performance and income tax rate are expected to offset a slight decline in its retail segment EBITDA margin rate.</font></p>
