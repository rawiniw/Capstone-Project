<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" color="#cc0000" face="Arial" style="color:#CC0000;font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Introduction</font></b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">This Conflict Minerals Report is being filed by Target Corporation (&#147;Target&#148;) pursuant to Rule&nbsp;13p-1 under the Securities Exchange Act and Form&nbsp;SD (collectively, the &#147;Conflict Minerals Rule&#148;). As used in this report, consistent with the Conflict Minerals Rule, &#147;3TG&#148; means columbite-tantalite (coltan), cassiterite, gold, wolframite and the derivatives tantalum, tin, and tungsten.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" color="#c00000" face="Arial" style="color:#C00000;font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">A.&#160; Applicability of the Conflict Minerals Rule&nbsp;to Target</font></b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">Target is a retailer selling both everyday essentials and fashionable, differentiated merchandise at discounted prices. Target does not manufacture any of the products it sells. A significant portion of Target&#146;s sales is from national brand merchandise. Approximately one-third of Target&#146;s fiscal 2016 sales related to owned and exclusive brand products. Target may be subject to the requirements of the Conflict Minerals Rule&nbsp;because of the degree of influence that it may exercise over the materials, parts, ingredients, or components of some of its owned and exclusive brand products that contain necessary 3TG.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">For 2016, for purposes of its compliance with the Conflict Minerals Rule, Target had potentially in-scope products in the owned and exclusive brand merchandise categories in the table below (the &#147;Surveyed Product Categories&#148;). As discussed herein, for 2016, Target&#146;s outreach included all of the vendors </font><font face="Arial">of products that were identified as possibly containing 3TG<font color="black" style="color:black;"> within the Surveyed Product Categories (the &#147;Surveyed Vendors&#148;), regardless of whether the products of a particular vendor were believed to contain necessary 3TG and regardless of whether Target may have contracted to manufacture those products.</font></font></p>

<div>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">The merchandise categories listed above are intended to summarize the types of owned and exclusive brand products Target sells that might be covered by its compliance with the Conflict Minerals Rule&nbsp;and, as a result, are different than the five product categories Target uses for purposes of its periodic reports filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission that cover all of the products it sells.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">For 2016, Target was unable to determine the origin of at least a portion of the necessary 3TG contained in the products of Surveyed Vendors. In some instances, Target was able to determine that some Surveyed Vendors for 2016 did not source from the Democratic Republic of the Congo (the &#147;DRC&#148;) or an adjoining country. Target&#146;s findings about the source of necessary 3TG in products from Surveyed Vendors for 2016 were limited by the fact that some vendors did not respond to Target&#146;s survey and that most vendors did not provide information about the source of necessary 3TG on an individual product basis, instead providing aggregate information for all of their products.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">Target does not do any direct business with any smelters or refiners of 3TG. As a &#147;downstream&#148; company (which means a company between the smelter or refiner and the consumer), it must rely on its vendors to provide accurate, reliable information about 3TG in its supply chain. Target also believes that, in most cases, it is several or more vendor tiers removed from the smelters and refiners of 3TG used in its products. However, through the efforts described in this Conflict Minerals Report, including its support of multi-stakeholder initiatives, Target is helping to address some of those difficulties presented to downstream companies and to encourage responsible sourcing in its supply chain.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" color="#c00000" face="Arial" style="color:#C00000;font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">B.&#160; Target&#146;s Conflict Minerals Policy; Participation in Industry Initiatives</font></b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">Target supports the humanitarian goals of the Conflict Minerals Rule&nbsp;and recognizes the adverse impact from mining and trade of 3TG by armed groups in the DRC and its adjoining countries. Target seeks to neither finance nor benefit those armed groups directly or indirectly.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">Target also recognizes that there are many smelters and refiners that source from the DRC and its adjoining countries whose activities do not finance or benefit armed groups and who are certified as &#147;Compliant&#148; (as defined in Annex A). In order to avoid the undue harm to local populations that a generalized embargo upon this region could perform, Target affirmatively encourages its suppliers to identify Compliant smelters and refiners within the DRC and its adjoining countries and to source from them when commercially practicable.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">Target&#146;s conflict minerals policy (the &#147;Conflict Minerals Policy&#148;) seeks to balance Target&#146;s objectives of ensuring that its business activities do not finance or benefit armed groups and providing support to Compliant smelters and refiners in the DRC and its adjoining countries. The Conflict Minerals Policy states that Target will not knowingly purchase or sell any owned or exclusive brand product that contains 3TG that finances armed conflict in the DRC or an adjoining country. The Conflict Minerals Policy also affirmatively encourages Target&#146;s suppliers to source from smelters and refiners within the DRC and its adjoining countries that are certified as Compliant.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Arial" style="color:black;font-size:10.0pt;">The Conflict Minerals Policy also includes, but is not limited to, Target&#146;s expectation that each of the vendors with which it contracts to manufacture will:</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
