 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><A NAME="tx662168_1"></A>ITEM&nbsp;5.02.&nbsp;DEPARTURE OF DIRECTORS OR CERTAIN OFFICERS; ELECTION OF
DIRECTORS; APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN OFFICERS; COMPENSATORY ARRANGEMENTS OF CERTAIN OFFICERS. </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On February&nbsp;25, 2019, the Board of
Directors of Amazon.com, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) elected Indra K. Nooyi as a director of the Company, and also appointed her to the Audit Committee of the Board. Ms.&nbsp;Nooyi was the Chief Executive Officer of PepsiCo, Inc. from October
2006 to October 2018, where she also served as the Chairman of its board of directors from May 2007 to February 2019. She was elected to PepsiCo&#146;s board of directors and became its President and Chief Financial Officer in 2001, and held
leadership roles in finance, corporate strategy and development, and strategic planning after joining PepsiCo in 1994. Ms.&nbsp;Nooyi has served as a director of Schlumberger Limited since April 2015. In connection with her election, Ms.&nbsp;Nooyi
was granted a restricted stock unit award under the Company&#146;s 1997 Stock Incentive Plan for 549 shares of common stock of the Company, to vest in three equal annual installments beginning on May&nbsp;15, 2020, assuming continued service as a
director. Ms.&nbsp;Nooyi also entered into an indemnification agreement with the Company in the same form its other directors have entered, which is filed as an exhibit to Amendment No.&nbsp;1, filed April&nbsp;21, 1997, to the Company&#146;s
Registration Statement on Form <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">S-1</FONT> (Registration <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">No.&nbsp;333-23795).</FONT> </P>
