<P style="MARGIN: 0pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman"></FONT>&nbsp;</P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; MARGIN: 0pt" align=center><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt" lang=EN-US color=black face="Times New Roman">RESTATED</FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; MARGIN: 0pt" align=center><B><U><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">Shareholder Rebuttal to the Amazon.com, Inc.</FONT></U></B></P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; MARGIN: 0pt" align=center><B><U><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">Opposition Statement Regarding Climate Change</FONT></U></B></P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; MARGIN: 0pt" align=center><B><U><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">(p. 13 of Amazon.com&#146;s Proxy)</FONT></U></B></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman"></FONT>&nbsp;</P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0pt"><B><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">Item # 6&#151;Shareholder Proposal</FONT></B></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman"></FONT>&nbsp;</P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">This proposal has been filed by Calvert Investment Management, Inc. (C.I.M.) on behalf of </FONT><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">Calvert Social Index Fund, Calvert Equity Portfolio, Calvert VP SRI Equity Portfolio, Calvert VP S&amp;P 500 Index Portfolio, and Calvert VP Nasdaq 100 Index Portfolio</FONT><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">. As an investor in Amazon.com, Inc. (&#147;Amazon&#148;) through these funds, C.I.M. is concerned with the company&#146;s lack of disclosure around its assessment and management of climate change. C.I.M. believes that </FONT><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US color=black face="Times New Roman">information from corporations on their climate change risks and management strategies is essential to investors as they assess the strengths of corporate securities in the context of climate change. All companies, regardless of industry, face potential challenges and opportunities from climate change. </FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US color=black face="Times New Roman"></FONT>&nbsp;</P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0pt"><B><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">Amazon&#146;s primary arguments against this proposal, as stated in its opposition statement, are that:&nbsp;</FONT></B></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman"></FONT>&nbsp;</P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: -18pt; MARGIN: 0pt 0pt 0pt 54pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 8pt" lang=EN-US face=Symbol>&#183;</FONT><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 7pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">They &#147;are aware of and are focused on the potential impacts of climate change, but [they] do not believe that preparing an ad hoc climate change report is an efficient use of time and resources.&#148; Furthermore, the company has already undertaken &#147;green&#148; initiatives, as demonstrated in its &#147;Amazon and Our Planet&#148; web page. </FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: -18pt; MARGIN-LEFT: 54pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 8pt" lang=EN-US face=Symbol>&#183;</FONT><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 7pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">&#147;E-commerce is inherently more environmentally friendly than traditional retailing&#148; and that the company &#147;will continue to innovate in this area over time.&#148;</FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0pt"><B><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">Our Rebuttal and Rationale for a YES vote follows:</FONT></B></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman"></FONT>&nbsp;</P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">This proposal calls on the company to disclose information on how it is assessing and managing climate change. We acknowledge and applaud the company&#146;s current initiatives to &#145;green&#146; some of its operations, but as investors, we are asking for specific data and qualitative information relating to its impact on climate change from activities that cause greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions, and how it is dealing with risks and opportunities from climate change related to the company. </FONT><B><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">Seventy percent of S&amp;P 500 companies and over 80 percent of Global 500 companies disclose this type of information through the Carbon Disclosure Project, including companies such as eBay Inc., Apple Inc., and Target Corp.</FONT></B><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman"> Many of these companies also choose to disclose key climate-related information through public websites and sustainability or corporate social responsibility reports. </FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman"></FONT>&nbsp;</P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: -18pt; MARGIN: 0pt 0pt 0pt 18pt"><B><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">Amazon&#146;s business has an impact on the environment, and on climate change in particular,</FONT></B></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0pt"><B><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">that is not currently addressed by the company through public disclosures. &nbsp; </FONT></B><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">The company&#146;s claim regarding the environmentally-friendliness of e-commerce fails to recognize key climate-related impacts related to Amazon&#146;s operations. </FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman"></FONT>&nbsp;</P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: -18pt; MARGIN: 0pt 0pt 0pt 36pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face=Symbol>&#183;</FONT><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 7pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">As a major company in the e-commerce industry and with a growing Web Services business, Amazon relies on data centers to process and store massive amounts of </FONT>


<FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; electronic information. Data centers require significant amounts of energy to power cooling system infrastructure.&nbsp; </FONT><B><U><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">Due to their electricity use, McKinsey &amp; Co estimates that data centers will be responsible for a greater portion of global GHG emissions than the aviation sector by 2020</FONT></U></B><B><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">.</FONT></B><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">&nbsp; According to recent reports from the company, Amazon plans to expand its cloud computing business, which inevitably creates a greater demand for data center operations. The company does not disclose any information on its data centers&#146; energy consumption, emissions, or related reduction plans.</FONT></P>

<P style="MARGIN: 0pt 0pt 0pt 18pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman"></FONT>&nbsp;</P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: -18pt; MARGIN: 0pt 0pt 0pt 36pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face=Symbol>&#183;</FONT><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 7pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">The Amazon Kindle e-reader, which the company self-reports to be its top selling product, is a consumer electronic product that is ostensibly made from an arrangement of plastics, metals, and chemicals. With regard to the Kindle, Amazon assumes the role of electronics developer and retailer, similar to Apple Inc. Apple products, such as the iPod and iPad, are manufactured by electronics manufacturer Foxconn, a company that is believed to be the manufacturer of the Amazon Kindle. Unlike Apple, however, Amazon does not disclose any information regarding the environmental footprint of its popular e-reader, including associated emissions from its production or use. </FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman"></FONT>&nbsp;</P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: -18pt; MARGIN: 0pt 0pt 0pt 36pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face=Symbol>&#183;</FONT><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 7pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt" lang=EN-US face="Times New Roman">Amazon may face a number of business risks due to climate change, none of which are addressed in public disclosures, including those related to regulation, energy and fuel costs, reputation, and physical impacts, which specifically could create challenges to the company&#146;s e-commerce business that relies on a reliable logistics network for products delivery. </FONT></P>
