<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>COSTCO WHOLESALE CORPORATION REPORTS FOURTH QUARTER AND </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>FISCAL YEAR 2017 OPERATING RESULTS AND SEPTEMBER SALES RESULTS </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">ISSAQUAH, Wash., October&nbsp;5, 2017- Costco Wholesale Corporation (&#147;Costco&#148; or the &#147;Company&#148;) (Nasdaq: COST) today announced its
operating results for the <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">17-week</FONT> fourth quarter and the <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">53-week</FONT> fiscal year ended September&nbsp;3, 2017. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Net sales for the <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">17-week</FONT> fourth quarter were $41.36&nbsp;billion, an increase of 15.8&nbsp;percent from
$35.73&nbsp;billion in the <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">16-week</FONT> fourth quarter of fiscal 2016. Net sales for the <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">53-week</FONT> fiscal year were $126.17&nbsp;billion, an increase of 8.7&nbsp;percent from
$116.07&nbsp;billion in the <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">52-week</FONT> fiscal year of 2016. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Company today also reported net sales of
$12.40&nbsp;billion for the month of September, the five weeks ended October&nbsp;1, 2017, an increase of 12.1&nbsp;percent from $11.06&nbsp;billion during the similar period last year. (The five-week period this year included the last week of the <FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">53-week</FONT> fiscal year 2017 ended September&nbsp;3, 2017). </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Comparable sales for the
