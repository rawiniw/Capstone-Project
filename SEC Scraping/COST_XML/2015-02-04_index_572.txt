<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>Approval of the Costco Wholesale Corporation 2002 Stock Incentive Plan as Amended and Restated </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">At the Annual Meeting of Shareholders (the &#147;Annual Meeting&#148;) of Costco Wholesale Corporation (the &#147;Company&#148;) held on
January&nbsp;29, 2015, shareholders approved the Company&#146;s 2002 Stock Incentive Plan as Amended and Restated, and renamed the Seventh Restated 2002 Incentive Plan (the &#147;Plan&#148;), which increases the number of shares of the
Company&#146;s common stock authorized for issuance under the Plan by 23.5&nbsp;million shares, subject to adjustment as described in the Plan, and extends the term of the Plan to December&nbsp;3, 2024. By approving the Plan, shareholders also
approved the material terms of the performance goals for performance-based awards under the Plan for purposes of Section&nbsp;162(m) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Plan may be administered by the Board, which has delegated plan administration authority to the Compensation Committee. Under the Plan,
employees, directors and individual consultants of the Company and its subsidiaries are eligible to receive awards. Awards may consist of options, stock unit awards and cash-based awards. A description of the material terms of the Plan is included
in the Company&#146;s definitive proxy statement on Schedule 14A for the Annual Meeting filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on December&nbsp;19, 2014 (the &#147;Proxy Statement&#148;). </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The foregoing summary of the Plan, and the summary of the Plan set forth in the Proxy Statement, are qualified in their entirety by reference
to the full text of the Plan, which is filed as Exhibit 10.1 to this Current Report and incorporated herein by reference. </P> <P STYLE="font-size:18pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>
