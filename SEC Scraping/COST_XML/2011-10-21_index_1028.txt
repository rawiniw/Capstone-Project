 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Item&nbsp;5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain
Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">(e) As previously disclosed, James Sinegal will retire as Chief
Executive Officer of the Company on December&nbsp;31, 2011, but will continue his employment with the Company until February&nbsp;1, 2013. On October&nbsp;18, 2011, the Compensation Committee of the Company&#146;s Board of Directors determined the
compensation arrangements for Mr.&nbsp;Sinegal during this transition. He will receive a pro rata portion of his annual salary (prescribed by contract as $350,000) until December&nbsp;31, 2011 while he serves as Chief Executive Officer and will be
eligible for an award of one-third of his normal bonus arrangement (a maximum of $66,667), to be determined by the Compensation Committee in January 2012. For the period January&nbsp;1, 2012, to February&nbsp;1, 2013, Mr.&nbsp;Sinegal will receive a
salary of $100,000 and will not be eligible for a bonus award. He will continue to receive his current employee benefits. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Mr. Sinegal was
awarded a grant of 8,333 restricted stock units. One-half will vest upon satisfaction of the performance conditions to be established by the Compensation Committee and the remaining half will vest at February&nbsp;1, 2013, when
Mr.&nbsp;Sinegal&#146;s service as an employee is concluded. </FONT></P>
