2013, to shareholders of record at the close of business on May&nbsp;17, 2013. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Costco currently operates 626 warehouses, including 449 in the

year on September&nbsp;1, 2013. Costco also operates electronic commerce web sites, at www.costco.com (U.S.), www.costco.ca (Canada), and www.costco.co.uk (United Kingdom). </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
