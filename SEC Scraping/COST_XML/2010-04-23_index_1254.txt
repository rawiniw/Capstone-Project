 <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>COSTCO WHOLESALE CORPORATION ANNOUNCES AN INCREASE IN </B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>ITS QUARTERLY CASH DIVIDEND </B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">ISSAQUAH, Wash., April&nbsp;22, 2010&#151;Costco Wholesale Corporation (Nasdaq: COST) today announced that its Board of Directors has
declared a quarterly cash dividend on Costco Wholesale common stock and approved a quarterly increase from $.18 to $.205 per share, or $.82 per share on an annualized basis. The dividend of $.205 per share is payable May&nbsp;21, 2010, to
shareholders of record at the close of business on May&nbsp;7, 2010. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Costco currently operates 567 warehouses, including 414
in the United States and Puerto Rico, 77 in Canada, 21 in the United Kingdom, seven in Korea, six in Taiwan, nine in Japan, one in Australia and 32 in Mexico. The Company also operates Costco Online, an electronic commerce web site, at
<U>www.costco.com</U> and at <U>www.costco.ca</U> in Canada. The Company plans to open an additional five to six new warehouses prior to the end of its 2010 fiscal year on August&nbsp;29, 2010. </FONT></P>
