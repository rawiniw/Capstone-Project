<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: right; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">Exhibit 99.1</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">Kroger and eCommerce Retailer Vitacost.com</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">Announce Definitive Merger Agreement</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; FONT-STYLE: italic; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Adds Online Technology and Fulfillment Platform to Accelerate Growth</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; FONT-STYLE: italic; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Into eCommerce Channel and New Markets</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; FONT-STYLE: italic; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Vitacost.com Shareholders to Receive $8.00 Per Share in Cash</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt"><font style="FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">CINCINNATI, OH and BOCA RATON, FL &#8211; July 2, 2014</font> &#8211; The Kroger Co. (NYSE: KR) and Vitacost.com, Inc. (NASDAQ: VITC), a leading online retailer of healthy living products, today announced a definitive merger agreement under which Kroger will purchase all outstanding shares of Vitacost.com for $8.00 per share in cash, or approximately $280 million.</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">The purchase price represents a premium of 51% to Vitacost.com&#8217;s closing share price on Feb. 19, 2014, the day before a major Vitacost.com shareholder publicly asked the company to evaluate strategic alternatives.&#160; The terms of the agreement were unanimously approved by the Boards of Directors of both companies.</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Under the terms of the merger agreement, Kroger will commence a tender offer for all of the outstanding shares of Vitacost.com common stock. Any shares of Vitacost.com common stock not acquired in the tender offer promptly will be acquired by Kroger in a subsequent merger. The tender offer and merger are subject to Vitacost.com stockholders tendering at least a majority of the outstanding shares of Vitacost.com common stock in the tender offer, certain regulatory approvals and other customary closing conditions.&#160; The transaction is not subject to any financing condition.&#160; Holders of approximately 26.2% of the outstanding shares of Vitacost.com common stock have agreed to support the transaction and tender their shares in the tender offer. The transaction is expected to close in the third calendar quarter of 2014.</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Vitacost.com brings to Kroger its strong position as one of the largest pure eCommerce companies in the nutrition and healthy living market, a team with extensive eCommerce experience, and a substantial platform that includes technology and ship-to-home fulfillment centers to serve customers in all 50 states and internationally.&#160; Vitacost.com offers more than 45,000 products including vitamins, minerals, herbs, supplements, sports nutrition, beauty care products and natural and organic foods to approximately 2.3 million active customers. The company has more than 800 associates and operates distribution centers in Lexington, NC and Las Vegas, NV, and a customer service center in Lexington, NC.</div>

<div style="MARGIN-TOP: 10pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 10pt; CLEAR: both" id="DSPFPageBreakArea">
<hr style="PAGE-BREAK-AFTER: always; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; BACKGROUND-COLOR: #b20000; MARGIN: 4px 0px; WIDTH: 100%; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; HEIGHT: 2px; COLOR: #b20000; CLEAR: both; BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px" id="DSPFPageBreak" noshade="noshade">
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">&#8220;We are delighted to welcome Vitacost.com to the Kroger family,&#8221; said Rodney McMullen, Kroger's chief executive officer.&#160; &#8220;Vitacost.com&#8217;s talented team has built an exceptional online retail destination in the growing nutrition and wellness market, with an enviable technology and fulfillment infrastructure. This merger is in line with our growth strategy to enter new markets and new channels, and &#8211; along with Harris Teeter&#8217;s online order and pick up service &#8211; accelerates our efforts to provide customers with even more ways to shop.&#160; Vitacost.com&#8217;s core focus on healthy living products is complementary to our fast-growing natural foods business, and we intend to grow Vitacost.com&#8217;s strong position in the online nutrition market.&#160; At the same time, we will build on Vitacost.com&#8217;s eCommerce platform by integrating it with our existing digital offerings to create exciting new levels of personalization and convenience for our customers.&#8221;</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">&#8220;We are excited to join such a tremendous organization and believe this will be an outstanding partnership,&#8221; stated Jeffrey Horowitz, Chief Executive Officer of Vitacost.com.&#160; &#8220;This transaction represents a significant premium for our shareholders and the company will benefit by leveraging Kroger&#8217;s scale and resources to further drive the online healthy living industry to new heights.&#8221;</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">Operational Highlights</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">&#160;</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Vitacost.com&#8217;s platform is highly complementary to Kroger&#8217;s existing businesses and enhances the company&#8217;s growth strategy.</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Vitacost.com&#8217;s leadership in nutrition and wellness products, including its proprietary brands, complements Kroger&#8217;s already fast-growing natural foods business and Simple Truth Organic and Simple Truth corporate brand offering.</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">The company&#8217;s eCommerce platform will enable Kroger to serve customers through ship-to-home orders in all 50 states, including 16 states that are currently not served by Kroger supermarkets, expanding Kroger&#8217;s reach into new US markets as well as internationally.&#160; Vitacost.com&#8217;s website and mobile app offer robust product information, rich content including recipes, videos and customer reviews, and &#8220;Set &amp; Save&#8221; subscriptions for thousands of items.</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Building on Vitacost.com&#8217;s technology and fulfillment platform and linking it to Kroger&#8217;s existing digital capabilities will accelerate Kroger&#8217;s omnichannel strategy.&#160; Millions of customers already plan their shopping online using Kroger&#8217;s cloud-based shopping list and weekly ad through the company&#8217;s mobile apps and Kroger.com, and more than one billion digital coupons have been downloaded since 2009.&#160; Kroger offers an order online, deliver-to-home service in its King Soopers division in Denver, and continues to learn from Harris Teeter&#8217;s successful Express Lane model, which offers customers the ability to order online and pick up at their local store in 154 locations.&#160; With Vitacost.com, Kroger will offer customers the convenience to order online and ship to their door with an expanded assortment online.</div>

<div style="MARGIN-TOP: 10pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 10pt; CLEAR: both" id="DSPFPageBreakArea">
<hr style="PAGE-BREAK-AFTER: always; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; BACKGROUND-COLOR: #b20000; MARGIN: 4px 0px; WIDTH: 100%; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; HEIGHT: 2px; COLOR: #b20000; CLEAR: both; BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px" id="DSPFPageBreak" noshade="noshade">
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Following closing, Vitacost.com will operate as a subsidiary of The Kroger Co. and continue to operate its facilities in Boca Raton, FL., Lexington N.C. and Las Vegas NV.</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">Financial Highlights</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">&#160;</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Kroger will finance the transaction with debt.&#160; The company expects to achieve a 2.00 &#8211; 2.20 net debt to EBITDA ratio by mid-to-late 2015.&#160; Kroger is committed to managing its free cash flow to achieve this goal in order to maintain its current investment grade credit rating.</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">The company also confirmed its net earnings per diluted share growth guidance range of $3.19 to $3.27 for fiscal 2014.&#160; Kroger expects to maintain its current long-term net earnings per diluted share growth rate of 8 &#8211; 11%, plus a growing dividend.</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Vitacost.com reported annual revenue of $382.7 million in fiscal 2013.&#160; Vitacost.com reported approximately 2.3 million total active customers as of the end of its first quarter of fiscal 2014, which ended March 31, and reported shipping 1.5 million orders.</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: ''Times New Roman'', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Weil, Gotshal &amp; Manges LLP is acting as legal advisor to Kroger.&#160; Jefferies LLC is acting as exclusive financial advisor to Vitacost.com.&#160; Wilson, Sonsini, Goodrich &amp; Rosati is acting as legal advisor to Vitacost.com.</div>
