(NYSE: RNDY) today announced the successful completion of Kroger&#146;s tender offer to purchase all outstanding shares of Roundy&#146;s common stock for $3.60 per share in cash. The tender offer expired at 11:59 p.m., Eastern Standard Time, on December&nbsp;17, 2015.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Kroger expects to complete the acquisition of the remaining eligible Roundy&#146;s shares not acquired in the tender offer later today through a merger under Section&nbsp;251(h)&nbsp;of the General Corporation Law of the State of Delaware.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;This merger blends Roundy&#146;s complementary markets with Kroger&#146;s strengths in scale and merchandising. Our future together is bright, and we look forward to learning from each other as partners,&#148; said Rodney McMullen, Kroger&#146;s chairman and CEO. &#147;Most of all, we want to welcome each of Roundy&#146;s more than 22,000 associates to the Kroger family of stores.&#148;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;We look forward to bringing together the best of Roundy&#146;s and Kroger for our customers and associates,&#148; said Bob Mariano, who will continue to lead Roundy&#146;s as president and CEO. He has served as chairman, president and chief executive officer of Roundy&#146;s since 2002. &#147;Our merger with Kroger will help us continue to exceed our customers&#146; expectations.&#148;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Additional Details of Tender Offer</font></b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">As of the expiration of the tender offer, approximately 33,144,128 shares were validly tendered and not withdrawn in the tender offer, representing 67.138 percent of Roundy&#146;s outstanding shares, according to the depositary. The condition to the tender offer that a majority of Roundy&#146;s outstanding shares on a fully-diluted basis be validly tendered and not withdrawn has been satisfied. As a result, Kroger has accepted for payment and will promptly pay for all validly tendered shares. The depositary has also informed Kroger that Notices of Guaranteed Delivery have been delivered with respect to 1,409,042 additional shares, representing approximately 2.854 percent of Roundy&#146;s currently outstanding shares.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">As a result of the merger, all remaining eligible Roundy&#146;s shares will be converted into the right to receive $3.60 per share in cash, without interest and less any applicable withholding taxes, the same price that was paid in the tender offer (eligible shares exclude (i)&nbsp;shares owned by Kroger, KS Merger Sub Inc. or Roundy&#146;s, (ii)&nbsp;shares held by any subsidiary of Roundy&#146;s or Kroger (other than KS Merger Sub Inc.) and (iii)&nbsp;shares held by Roundy&#146;s stockholders who properly demand appraisal under Delaware law).</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Following completion of the merger, Roundy&#146;s shares will cease to be traded on the New York Stock Exchange, which is expected to take effect later today.</font></p>
