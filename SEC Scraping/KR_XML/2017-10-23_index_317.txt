<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Kroger Names Executive Leadership Team to <br> Support Restock Kroger Plan</font></b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><i><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-style:italic;">EVP of Retail Operations Fred Morganthall to Retire;</font></i></p>
<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><i><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-style:italic;">EVP of Merchandising Mike Donnelly Named EVP and COO</font></i></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">CINCINNATI, October&nbsp;23, 2017</font></b> &#151; The Kroger Co. (NYSE: KR) today announced its executive leadership team to support the company&#146;s <i>Restock Kroger Plan</i>.</p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;Kroger is fortunate to have a leadership team that combines deep experience with creative new talent as we strategically reposition the company through our <i>Restock Kroger Plan</i>,&#148; said Rodney McMullen, Kroger&#146;s chairman and CEO.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Fred Morganthall, Kroger&#146;s executive vice president of retail operations, will retire after 31 years of distinguished service, effective November&nbsp;30. Mike Donnelly, currently Kroger&#146;s executive vice president of merchandising, will assume responsibility for both operations and merchandising as executive vice president and chief operating officer, effective November&nbsp;1.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Kroger&#146;s key lines of business continue to be led by three executive vice presidents who are driving execution of <i>Restock Kroger</i>: Mr.&nbsp;Donnelly, Mike Schlotman, who serves as executive vice president and chief financial officer, and Chris Hjelm, who serves as executive vice president and chief information officer.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Fred Morganthall to Retire after 31 Years of Service<br>  </font></b>Mr.&nbsp;Morganthall will retire from the company after 31 years of service, and 44 years in the food industry.</p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;There are few people in our industry as widely respected as Fred,&#148; said Mr.&nbsp;McMullen. &#147;He worked tirelessly to establish the Harris Teeter brand and, after our merger, to help position Kroger for future success. We&#146;ve all benefited from Fred&#146;s passion for grocery retail and operational excellence. We are grateful for Fred&#146;s distinguished service and many contributions to Kroger and Harris Teeter, and we wish him and his family all the best.&#148;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Mr.&nbsp;Morganthall&#146;s professional life began at Procter&nbsp;&amp; Gamble in 1973. His career in grocery retail began in 1978 at Spartan Stores in Grand Rapids, Mich., where he was responsible for</font></p>

In 1986, he joined Harris Teeter as director of grocery merchandising. With Harris Teeter, Mr.&nbsp;Morganthall served in a number of executive management positions, including vice president of merchandising, vice president of distribution and vice president of operations before being named president in 1997. Under his leadership, Harris Teeter grew and firmly established its identity as a retailer that provides exceptional customer service and in-store experience.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">After the 2013 merger of Kroger and Harris Teeter, Mr.&nbsp;Morganthall was named Kroger&#146;s senior vice president in June&nbsp;2015. He was promoted to his current role, executive vice president of retail operations, in September&nbsp;2015.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Mr.&nbsp;Morganthall has been an active leader in industry organizations, including serving as chair of the Food Marketing Institute. <p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Mr.&nbsp;Morganthall and his wife, Judy, plan to spend time with family, especially grandchildren, and look forward to traveling together.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Mr.&nbsp;Donnelly, Kroger&#146;s executive vice president of merchandising, will assume responsibility for the company&#146;s operations and merchandising as EVP and COO, effective November&nbsp;1.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;Mike and his team will help us redefine the food and grocery experience for customers and drive sales,&#148; said Mr.&nbsp;McMullen. &#147;By bringing merchandising and operations together under Mike&#146;s leadership, we will build synergies between our teams and accelerate our efforts to establish a truly seamless customer experience.&#148;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Mr.&nbsp;Donnelly began his career at Fry&#146;s Food Stores in California in 1978. He was named vice president of merchandising for Fry&#146;s in 1995 and promoted to president of the Fry&#146;s division in 2000. He has served as Kroger&#146;s senior vice president of drug/GM, president of the company&#146;s Ralphs division, and senior vice president of merchandising before being promoted to his current position in 2015.</font></p>
