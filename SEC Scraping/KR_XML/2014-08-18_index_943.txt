 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="right"><B>Exhibit (a)(5)(h) </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:16pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Kroger Completes Merger with eCommmerce Retailer Vitacost.com </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:12pt; font-family:Times New Roman">CINCINNATI, OH and BOCA RATON, FL &#150; August&nbsp;18, 2014 &#150; The Kroger Co. (NYSE: KR) and Vitacost.com, Inc. (NASDAQ: VITC), a leading online
retailer of healthy living products, today announced the successful completion of Kroger&#146;s tender offer to purchase all outstanding shares of common stock of Vitacost.com for $8.00 per share in cash. The tender offer expired at 5:00 p.m.,
Eastern Daylight Time, on August&nbsp;15, 2014. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:12pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Kroger expects to complete the acquisition of the remaining eligible Vitacost.com shares not acquired in
the tender offer later today through a merger under Section&nbsp;251(h) of the General Corporation Law of the State of Delaware. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:12pt; font-family:Times New Roman">As of the expiration of
the tender offer, approximately 29,842,390 shares were validly tendered and not withdrawn in the tender offer, representing 86.68 percent of Vitacost.com&#146;s outstanding shares, according to the depositary. The condition to the tender offer that
a majority of Vitacost.com&#146;s outstanding shares on a fully diluted basis be validly tendered and not withdrawn has been satisfied. As a result, Kroger has accepted for payment and will promptly pay for all validly tendered shares. The
depositary has also informed Kroger that Notices of Guaranteed Delivery have been delivered with respect to 836,275 additional shares, representing approximately 2.43 percent of Vitacost.com&#146;s currently outstanding shares. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:12pt; font-family:Times New Roman">As a result of the merger, all remaining eligible Vitacost.com shares will be converted into the right to receive $8.00 per share in cash, without interest
and less any applicable withholding taxes, the same price that was paid in the tender offer (eligible shares exclude (i)&nbsp;shares owned by Kroger, Vigor Acquisition Corp. (Kroger&#146;s wholly owned subsidiary that made the offer) or
Vitacost.com, (ii)&nbsp;shares held by any subsidiary of Vitacost.com or Kroger (other than Vigor Acquisition Corp.) and (iii)&nbsp;shares held by Vitacost.com stockholders who properly demand appraisal under Delaware law). </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:12pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Following completion of the merger, Vitacost.com shares will cease to be traded on the NASDAQ Global Market, which is expected to take effect later today.
</P> <P STYLE="font-size:12pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P> <P STYLE="margin-top:24pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:12pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Kroger, one of the world&#146;s largest retailers, employs more than 375,000
associates who serve customers in 2,640 supermarkets and multi-department stores in 34 states and the District of Columbia under two dozen local banner names including Kroger, City Market, Dillons, Food 4 Less, Fred Meyer, Fry&#146;s, Harris Teeter,
Jay C, King Soopers, QFC, Ralphs and Smith&#146;s. The company also operates 786 convenience stores, 320 fine jewelry stores, 1,240 supermarket fuel centers and 38 food processing plants in the U.S. Recognized by Forbes as the most generous company
in America, Kroger supports hunger relief, breast cancer awareness, the military and their families, and more than 30,000 schools and grassroots organizations. Kroger contributes food and funds equal to 200&nbsp;million meals a year through more
than 80 Feeding America food bank partners. </P>
