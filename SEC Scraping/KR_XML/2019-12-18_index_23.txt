<P STYLE="font: 14pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><B>Kroger Elects Karen M. Hoguet to Board
of Directors</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 14pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><FONT STYLE="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>CINCINNATI, December 12, 2019 </B>&ndash; The Kroger Co.
(NYSE: KR) today announced that Karen M. Hoguet has been elected to the company&rsquo;s board of directors.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Ms. Hoguet served as the chief financial officer of Macy&rsquo;s,
Inc. from October 1997 until July 2018. She then served as a strategic advisor to the CEO until her retirement earlier this year.
Ms. Hoguet currently serves on the Nielsen Holdings plc board and previously served on the boards of Chubb and Cincinnati Bell.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&ldquo;We are pleased to welcome Karen to Kroger,&rdquo; said
Rodney McMullen, Kroger&rsquo;s chairman and CEO. &ldquo;She brings more than 35 years of retail and commercial experience to our
board. Karen&rsquo;s long tenure as a senior executive of a publicly-traded retail company provides strategy, transformation, financial,
audit and risk oversight experience that will benefit our business and our shareholders.&rdquo;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Ms. Hoguet is a graduate of Brown University, receiving a Sc.B.
in Applied Mathematics. She earned an MBA from Harvard Business School. Ms. Hoguet was recognized by the <I>Wall Street Journal</I>
as the nation&rsquo;s top CFO in 2014. She gives back to the Cincinnati community through her service as a member of the UC Health
and Hebrew Union College boards.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Ms. Hoguet is elected to serve until Kroger&rsquo;s annual meeting
of shareholders in June 2020. At that time, she will stand for election by the shareholders.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>About Kroger</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">At The Kroger Co. (NYSE: KR), we are Fresh
for Everyone&trade; and dedicated to our Purpose: To&nbsp;Feed the Human Spirit&reg;. We are, across our family of companies,
nearly half a million associates who serve over 11 million customers daily through a seamless shopping experience under a variety
of&nbsp;<U>banner names</U>. We are committed to creating #ZeroHungerZeroWaste communities by 2025. To learn more about us, visit
our&nbsp;<U>newsroom</U>&nbsp;and investor relations&nbsp;<U>site</U>.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>
