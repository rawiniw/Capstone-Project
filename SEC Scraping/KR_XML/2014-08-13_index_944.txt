 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">This Amendment No.&nbsp;2 (this &#147;Amendment&#148;) amends and supplements the Tender Offer
Statement on Schedule TO filed by The Kroger Co., an Ohio corporation (&#147;Kroger&#148;), and Vigor Acquisition Corp., a Delaware corporation (&#147;Purchaser&#148;) and a wholly-owned subsidiary of Kroger, with the Securities and Exchange
Commission on July&nbsp;18, 2014 (together with any subsequent amendments and supplements thereto, the &#147;Schedule TO&#148;). The Schedule TO relates to the offer by Purchaser to purchase all of the issued and outstanding shares of common stock,
par value $0.00001 per share (the &#147;Shares&#148;), of Vitacost.com, Inc., a Delaware corporation (the &#147;Company&#148;), at a purchase price of $8.00 per Share, net to the holder thereof in cash, without interest thereon and subject to any
required tax withholding, upon the terms and subject to the conditions set forth in the Offer to Purchase, dated July&nbsp;18, 2014 (the &#147;Offer to Purchase&#148;), a copy of which is attached as Exhibit (a)(1)(A) to the Schedule TO, and in the
related Letter of Transmittal (the &#147;Letter of Transmittal&#148;), a copy of which is attached as Exhibit (a)(1)(B) to the Schedule TO, which, together with any amendments or supplements thereto, collectively constitute the &#147;Offer&#148;.
</P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">All information contained in the Offer to Purchase and the accompanying Letter of Transmittal, including all schedules thereto, is hereby
incorporated herein by reference in response to Items 1 through 9 and Item&nbsp;11 in the Schedule TO. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">This Amendment is being filed to
amend and supplement Item&nbsp;11 as reflected below. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><I>Item&nbsp;11 Additional Information. </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Item&nbsp;11 of the Schedule TO is hereby amended and supplemented as follows: </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The subsection entitled &#147;Legal Proceedings&#148; of Section&nbsp;16 &#151; &#147;Certain Legal Matters; Regulatory Approvals&#148; of the
Offer to Purchase is hereby amended and restated in its entirety to read as follows: </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:8%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;Following the July&nbsp;2, 2014 announcement
that the Company had entered into the Merger Agreement, three putative class actions were filed on behalf of purported stockholders of the Company against the Company, its directors, Kroger and Purchaser (collectively, the &#147;Defendants&#148;).
One action was filed in the Circuit Court of the Fifteenth Judicial Circuit In and For Palm Beach County, Florida: <I>John Ernst v. Vitacost.com, Inc., et al.</I>, 2014-CA-008318, filed on July&nbsp;7, 2014 (the &#147;<I>Ernst</I> Action&#148;). Two
actions were filed in the Court of Chancery of the State of Delaware: <I>Marcel Heim v. Vitacost.com, Inc., et al.</I>, C.A. No.&nbsp;9883-VCP, filed on July&nbsp;15, 2014 (the &#147;<I>Heim</I> Action&#148;), and <I>Takis P. Dionisos v.
Vitacost.com, et al.</I>, C.A. No.&nbsp;9945-VCP, filed on July&nbsp;24, 2014 (the &#147;<I>Dionisos</I> Action&#148; and, together with the <I>Ernst</I> Action and the <I>Heim</I> Action, the &#147;Stockholder Actions&#148;). </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:8%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Each of the Stockholder Actions alleges that the Company&#146;s directors breached their fiduciary duties to the Company&#146;s stockholders
in connection with their consideration and approval of the Merger Agreement by, among other things, agreeing to an inadequate tender price through an inadequate sales process. The Stockholder Actions also allege that the Company, Kroger and
Purchaser aided and abetted the Company&#146;s directors&#146; alleged breaches of fiduciary duty. The <I>Dionisos</I> Action alleges that the Company&#146;s directors failed to disclose material information concerning the Offer. The Stockholder
Actions seek, among other things, an injunction to prevent consummation of the Offer and subsequent Merger. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:8%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On July&nbsp;24, 2014, the
plaintiff in the <I>Dionisos</I> Action filed a motion for a preliminary injunction. On July&nbsp;31, 2014, the <I>Heim</I> Action and the<I> Dionisos</I> Action were consolidated. A hearing on the motion for a preliminary injunction was set for
August&nbsp;11, 2014 in the Delaware Court of Chancery, and as set forth below, such motion has since been withdrawn. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:8%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On August 12, 2014,
the parties to the Stockholder Actions entered into a memorandum of understanding (the &#147;Memorandum of Understanding&#148;) to settle the Stockholder Actions. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:8%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Memorandum of Understanding provides that, among other things, the Company made additional disclosures and took certain actions contained
in Amendment No.&nbsp;2 to the Schedule 14D-9 filed with the SEC on August&nbsp;8, 2014.&nbsp;Pursuant to the agreement of the parties, the motion for a preliminary injunction in the Stockholder Actions was withdrawn and the hearing relating thereto
