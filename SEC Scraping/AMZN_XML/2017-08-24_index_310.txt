<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>Amazon and Whole Foods Market Announce Acquisition to Close This Monday, </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>Will Work Together to Make High-Quality, Natural and Organic Food Affordable for Everyone </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">August&nbsp;24, 2017&#151;Seattle, Wash. &amp; Austin, Texas&#151;(NASDAQ: AMZN)&#151;Amazon and Whole Foods Market&nbsp;today announced that Amazon&#146;s
acquisition of Whole Foods Market will close on Monday August&nbsp;28, 2017, and the two companies will together pursue the vision of making Whole Foods Market&#146;s high-quality, natural and organic food affordable for everyone. As a down payment
on that vision, Whole Foods Market will offer lower prices starting Monday on a selection of best-selling grocery staples across its stores, with more to come. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In addition, Amazon and Whole Foods Market technology teams will begin to integrate Amazon Prime into the Whole Foods Market <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap"><FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">point-of-sale</FONT></FONT> system, and when this work is complete, Prime members will receive special savings and <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">in-store</FONT> benefits.&nbsp;The two companies will invent in additional
areas over time, including in merchandising and logistics, to enable lower prices for Whole Foods Market customers. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;We&#146;re determined to make
healthy and organic food affordable for everyone. Everybody should be able to eat Whole Foods Market quality &#150; we will lower prices without compromising Whole Foods Market&#146;s long-held commitment to the highest standards,&#148; said Jeff
Wilke, CEO of Amazon Worldwide Consumer. &#147;To get started, we&#146;re going to lower prices beginning Monday on a selection of best-selling grocery staples, including Whole Trade organic bananas, responsibly-farmed salmon, organic large brown
eggs, <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">animal-welfare-rated</FONT> 85% lean ground beef, and more. And this is just the beginning &#150; we will make Amazon Prime the customer rewards program at Whole Foods Market and continuously lower prices as we
invent together. There is significant work and opportunity ahead, and we&#146;re thrilled to get started.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;It&#146;s been our mission for 39
years at Whole Foods Market to bring the highest quality food to our customers,&#148; said John Mackey, Whole Foods Market <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">co-founder</FONT> and CEO. &#147;By working together with Amazon and integrating in several
key areas, we can lower prices and double down on that mission and reach more people with Whole Foods Market&#146;s high-quality, natural and organic food. As part of our commitment to quality, we&#146;ll continue to expand our efforts to support
and promote local products and suppliers. We can&#146;t wait to start showing customers what&#146;s possible when Whole Foods Market and Amazon innovate together.&#148; </P>
