<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Item 5.02.&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; <u>Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers</u>.</font></b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><u><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Election of Directors</font></u></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On March&nbsp;13, 2013, the Board of Directors elected Douglas M. Baker,&nbsp;Jr. and Henrique De Castro as directors of Target Corporation.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Mr.&nbsp;Baker is Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Ecolab Inc.&#160; Mr.&nbsp;De Castro is Chief Operating Officer of Yahoo! Inc.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The Board also appointed Mr.&nbsp;Baker to Target&#146;s Audit Committee and Nominating&nbsp;&amp; Governance Committee, and appointed Mr.&nbsp;De Castro to Target&#146;s Corporate Responsibility Committee and Nominating&nbsp;&amp; Governance Committee.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Messrs.&nbsp;Baker and De Castro each received a one-time grant of RSUs with a $50,000 grant date fair value and will receive the annual compensation we provide to non-employee directors described in our most recent proxy statement under the heading &#147;Director Compensation.&#148;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">A copy of the News Release announcing these changes to the Board of Directors is attached hereto as Exhibit&nbsp;(99)A.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><u><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Retirement of Director</font></u></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Stephen W. Sanger retired from the Board of Directors on March&nbsp;13, 2013 in connection with reaching the five-year post-retirement term limit under our Corporate Governance Guidelines.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><u><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Retirement of Executive Officer</font></u></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On March&nbsp;13, 2013 Target Corporation announced that Terrence J. Scully, President, Target Financial and Retail Services, will move to a strategic advisory role with Target on April&nbsp;1, 2013 and plans to retire in March&nbsp;2014.&#160; Accordingly, Mr.&nbsp;Scully will no longer be classified as an executive officer of Target after April&nbsp;1, 2013.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">A copy of the News Release announcing this event is attached hereto as Exhibit&nbsp;(99)B.</font></p>
