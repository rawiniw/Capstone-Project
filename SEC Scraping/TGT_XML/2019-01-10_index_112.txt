<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">3.</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>The Company agrees to bear the cost of (i)&nbsp;the arbitrator&#146;s fee, and (ii)&nbsp;any other expense or cost Executive would not be required to bear if Executive were free to bring the dispute or claim in court. Each party shall bear their own attorneys&#146; fees incurred in connection with the arbitration. The arbitrator will not have authority to award attorneys&#146; fees unless a statute or contract at issue in the dispute authorizes the award of attorneys&#146; fees to the prevailing party. In such case, the arbitrator shall have the authority to make an award of attorneys&#146; fees as required or permitted by the applicable statute or contract.</p>
